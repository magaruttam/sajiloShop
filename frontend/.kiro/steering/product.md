# Product

This is the frontend for **SajiloShop** — an e-commerce platform targeting the Nepali market. The app allows users to browse products by category, view trending items, discover personalized recommendations, and manage a shopping cart.

Key user-facing sections:
- Hero banner with image carousel
- Product categories
- "Trending in Nepal" section
- "Just For You" personalized recommendations
- Product cards with cart functionality (in progress)
